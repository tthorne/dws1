Element Definitions

h1
Level one header used for main headings.
h2
Level two header used for sub headings.
h3
Level three header.
h4
Level four header.
h5
Level five header.
h6
Level six header.
p
Creates a paragraph
b
Characters appear as Bold
i
Characters appear as Italic
sup
Characters that should be superscript such as the suffixes of dates or mathematical concepts.
sub
Used to contain characters that should be subscript.
br
Adds a line break inside the middle of a paragraph.
hr
Adds a horizontal rule between sections.
strong
Indicates that its content has strong importance. Characters will appear as bold.
em
Indicates emphasis that subtly changes the meaning of a sentence. Characters will apear as italic
blockquote
Used to indicate longer quotes that take up an entire paragraph.
q
Used to indicate shorter quotes that sit within a paragraph.
abbr
Indicated an abbreviation or an acronym.
cite
Used when referencing piece of work such as book, film or research paper.
dfn
Indicates the defining of a new term.
address
Contains details of the author of the page. Can include address, phone number and email address.
ins
Shows content that has been added into the document.
del
Shows content that has been deleted into the document.
s
Indicates information that no longer accurate or relevant. Appears with a line the center.
